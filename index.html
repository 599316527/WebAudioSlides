Why another audio API?

We have <audio> already!


<audio> hides the steps of loading, decoding and playing

        <audio controls preload="auto" src="mysound.ogg"> </audio>

Web Audio provides:


  • Precise timing of lots of overlapping sounds
  • An audio pipeline and routing system for effects and filters
  • Hooks to visualize and manipulate audio data on the fly

Building Simple Game Audio is easy


  • Load audio files with XHR
  • Tell Web Audio to decode them into buffers
  • Create source node, point at buffer, and  start() !
